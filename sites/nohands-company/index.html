What we believe

Software for everyone, forever

🌐

The internet should be open

Too much of the internet runs on servers owned by a handful of companies. We are building an alternative where many independent operators share the responsibility.

🤝

No lock-in, ever

Every piece of software we make can be self-hosted by anyone. If we disappear tomorrow, you keep your data and the code is still there.

🔓

Open source by default

All of our code is public on GitHub under permissive licenses. You are free to read it, fork it, run it, and contribute to it.

👤

Designed for real people

Our tools are not just for developers. We design for people who want to publish a website, share a file, or log in without a PhD in computer science.

Who we are

A small team
with a big idea

The No Hands Company is an independent software studio. We build tools for a healthier internet — one where no single organisation has too much control.

We are small on purpose. Every project we release is something we use ourselves. We do not chase growth metrics or venture capital. We chase good software.
